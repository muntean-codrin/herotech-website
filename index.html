ABOUT US PROJECTS

WHO ARE WE?

We are Herotech, a team of 4 passionate and young students that believe that things can be done differently. We dream for a better future, a more efficient way to use everyday objects, a method to make life just a little bit different. We strive for robotic assistance, for a future in which our human barriers can be overcome.

The goal

We need electronic circuits that meet certain requirements, depending on the situation

Electronic Schematic

Using Autodesk Eagle, our team designs complex electrical schematics for circuits used in our projects

PCB Layout

We design the trace for components according to the electrical schematics

PCB Prototyping

We usually make our PCB-s in specialized factories, but sometimes we have to make them ourselves

Testing

After we solder all the required components, we verify that the circuit works in normal parameters

Future improvements

If necessary, we modify our circuits and work on their integration into our projects

The goal

Our goal is to develop the best software solutions and integrate them in our projects.

The key

To get the best software product, we develop more solutions, than we test them and compare the results.

Project diagram

Before we start programming, we create a project diagram, which help us stay organised during the next steps.

The code

To write the code we are using IDE-s like Android Studio, Arduino, Atollic Studio, Keil, Visual Stuio based on the project that we are working on.

Measurements

In order to create some specific parts that we need in our projects, we have to take a series of measurements, then we create the components based on them.

3D Design

Based on certain requirements, we design our components using Autodesk Fusion 360.

Simulation

In order to achieve the best results, we simulate the behaviour of our objects in different situations.

3D Printing

Using the FDM technology, we have the possibility to create unique parts and our digital objects turn into real components.

FTC Ultimate Goal

FTC is a competition for which we have to create a robot that fulfills certain tasks set by the organizers. This robotics competition takes place in several stages: regional, national and global (Detroit, USA).

Cansat 2021

A CanSat is a simulation of a real satellite, integrated within the volume and shape of a soft drink can. The team’s challenge is to fit all the major subsystems of a satellite inside this minimal volume, launched by a small rocket up to an altitude of 1 km.
